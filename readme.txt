Easily add the Smartwaiver Waiver Widget to your WordPress site so customers can sign your digital waiver without ever leaving your site.

== Description ==

[Smartwaiver](https://www.smartwaiver.com/) is an online waiver solution that converts your existing paper waiver into an interactive digital document. The Smartwaiver Waiver Widget for WordPress allows you to easily add your digital waiver as a small pop-up widget on the bottom of your site so customers can complete and sign your waiver directly from your website. 

All you need is a Smartwaiver account and the plugin.

In your [Smartwaiver account](https://www.smartwaiver.com/m/user/sw_login.php?wms_create_new_user
) you’ll be able to customize the look and feel of your widget to match your WordPress site.

== Installation ==

For an automatic installation through WordPress: 

1. Under the plugins tab, select 'Add New' in your WordPress admin page.
2. Search for ‘Smartwaiver Waiver Widget’ in the plugin search box.
3. Click ‘Install Now’ and activate the plugin.
4. Navigate to your admin Settings and click on “Smartwaiver”.
5. Copy and paste the HTML code from your [Smartwaiver account](https://www.smartwaiver.com/kb/166/) into the text box in WordPress.

== Screenshots ==

1. Click on **Plugins** and then **Add New**.
2. Search for **Smartwaiver Waiver Widget** then click **Install Now**. Click **Activate Plugin**.
3. Login to Smartwaiver. Go to the [Waiver Widget Editor](https://www.smartwaiver.com/m/webpl/webpl.php?webpl_create). Customize the look and feel of your widget and then **copy the HTML** code from the box on the right of the screen.
4. Go back to your WordPress admin page and click on **Settings > Smartwaiver**. Select the page where the Waiver Widget will appear.
5. Now **paste the HTML code**, copied from Smartwaiver, into the text box and click **Update Waiver Widget**.
6. Confirm your Waiver Widget is appearing on your site's public pages.
 
== Frequently Asked Questions ==
 
= How do I access my Smartwaiver JavaScript code to add to my WordPress plugin? =
 
Use Smartwaiver’s [custom widget tool](https://www.smartwaiver.com/m/webpl/webpl.php?webpl_create) to create your custom JavaScript.  
 
= Where can I find more detailed help? =

There are full instructions at the [Smartwaiver Help Center](https://support.smartwaiver.com/hc/en-us/articles/115015595727).

= Can I choose which pages show my Smartwaiver Waiver Widget? =

Yes. You can select a single page, or show your waiver widget on all pages.
